Session Information

Lead with AI, Grow with Purpose

Event: IEEE WIE International Leadership Summit 2025

Theme Alignment: "Lead with Purpose: Nurturing and Growing Together Towards a Sustainable Tomorrow"

Session Objectives

  • Introduce 20 AI tools categorized by professional persona
  • Provide practical implementation strategies for immediate adoption
  • Demonstrate productivity gains through real-world use cases
  • Foster AI literacy while maintaining academic and professional integrity

Expected Outcomes

  • Participants will identify 3-5 tools relevant to their persona
  • Gain copy-paste ready prompts for immediate implementation
  • Understand the compound effect of daily AI-powered improvements
  • Develop a 30-day AI adoption roadmap
