Quick answers.

Who is Shankar J?

A PhD scholar at NIT Calicut researching brain-computer interfaces and EEG deep learning, and a keynote, FDP and workshop speaker on AI/ML in signal processing who has delivered 481 expert sessions to more than 16,500 learners. Four-time IEEE-recognised volunteer leader.

What topics does Shankar speak on?

AI/ML in signal processing and EEG, brain-computer interfaces, applied machine learning for biomedical data, AI-assisted research workflows for scholars and faculty, and LinkedIn positioning for technical professionals, delivered as keynotes, FDPs and hands-on workshops, online or in person.

How do I book him for a keynote, FDP or workshop?

Send your event date, audience profile and topic via the booking page, WhatsApp or LinkedIn. He replies within 24 hours with fit, availability and a tailored quote, available across India and online worldwide.

Is Shankar open to research roles?

Yes. Research Scientist and Research Associate roles in biomedical AI, EEG and brain-computer interfaces, in industry or academia. Remote-first; for on-site positions he is based in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. See the research profile.
