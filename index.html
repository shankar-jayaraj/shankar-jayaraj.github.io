Shankar is currently a PhD Scholar under the Electronics and Communication Engineering Department of the National Institute of Technology, Calicut. His research area is in biomedical signal processing. He was working as Assistant Professor in the Electronics and Communication Engineering Department at A P J Abdul Kalam Technological University (formerly Kerala Technological University) with a total of 8.5 years of teaching and research experience and two years of experience in the telecom industry.


He completed his B.Tech in Electronics and Communication from the University College of Engineering (UCE), Kariavattom (University of Kerala), in 2009 After two years of industry experience in the telecom sector. He completed his M.Tech in Communication Systems from SRM University, Chennai, in 2013. His paper “Combined Utility and Adaptive Residence Time Based Network Selection algorithm for 4G Wireless Networks” received the Springer best paper award during the 3rd International Conference on Multimedia Technology (ICMT 2013), Guangzhou, China. In addition, it was published in Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering as a Springer Book Chapter.


He is an active IEEE Volunteer, a Mentor of Change of Atal Tinkering Lab, AIM, NITI Aayog, Govt of India, and a licensed Amateur Radio Operator. He has won several awards for his volunteer contributions to students and the academic community.


He's a forward-thinking expert, navigating the latest technological frontiers, from AI, ML, Generative AI, and AI in smart health to AI productivity tools, IoT, Robotics, 3D Printing, Cryptocurrencies, NFTs, Web3 apps, RPA, Satellite Tracking, Software Defined Radio, and Amateur Radio, exemplifying his visionary command of cutting-edge tech trends, with expertise in Prompt Engineering using Large Language models like GPT-4 (ChatGPT), Bard, and Claude.


He is an AI productivity strategist, with experience & access to paid version of perplexity, Paperpal, R discovery, Mind the graph along with free AI and digital tools.
